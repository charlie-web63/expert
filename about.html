About LED App

Purpose

The LED App is designed to help users control and customize LED lighting systems with ease. Whether you are managing home decor, creating ambient lighting, or experimenting with colors, this app provides a simple and intuitive interface for all your LED needs.

Features

  • Remote control of LED lights
  • Customizable color palettes
  • Brightness and pattern adjustments
  • Scheduling and automation options
  • Support for multiple LED devices

How It Works

Connect your LED devices to the app via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. Use the dashboard to select colors, set patterns, and schedule lighting events. The app communicates directly with your hardware for real-time updates and control.
